Mobile Legends

Guinevere

Guinevere is a very powerful, almost imbalanced hero in Mobile Legends: Bang Bang. As a Mage and Fighter hero, she can deal massive amounts of magic damage and stun and disable them for a few seconds, all while being immune to stuns and disables!

Lesley

Lesley is the Queen of Crit damage and can wreck the whole enemy team by using her abilities in Mobile Legends. Because of her massive critical damage, she can literally kill any enemy ADR(squishy heroes i.e. MM/Mage) with just 2 shots. And a secret fact- Harley is Lesley's brother.

Lylia

Hero Feature A Mage who can return to her position a few seconds ago and deal high damage through a series of explosions.Lylia gets power from Gloom, increasing Movement Speed by 15%. Each time Gloom levels up, she will increase extra Movement Speed by 5%.
